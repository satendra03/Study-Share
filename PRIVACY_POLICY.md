# Privacy Policy (Draft)

This Privacy Policy describes basic data handling practices for StudyShare.
This is a minimal policy intended to accompany the repository; projects deploying
StudyShare should replace this draft with a full, jurisdiction-appropriate policy.

## Data Collection
- StudyShare may collect account information (email, name), uploaded content metadata
  (file names, subjects), and usage metrics.

## Use of Data
- Data is used to provide platform functionality (authentication, file storage,
  discovery/search) and to improve the service.

## Storage & Retention
- Uploaded files may be stored with third-party providers (e.g., Cloudinary) as
  configured by the deployment. Retention policies depend on the deployment settings.

## Third-Party Services
- Integrations (Cloudinary, Gemini AI) are governed by their own privacy policies.
